WORKS

Live Scribbling Digi-pen is a helpful product that satisfies the needs of the people in today’s technologic and fast life. This product can be used in many ways. The Digi-pen provides taking fast and easy notes to people who are busy one’s self with something. As we scribble on any paper, the movements will be recorded and sent to the wireless device connected to it. The recorded movements will be analysed and corresponding text will be displayed. An audio will also be played that corresponds to the text. This will save time and facilitate life.
